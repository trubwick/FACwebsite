About Me

Based in Brighton, UK, I am an experienced design engineer with experience working on all kinds of projects, from major high-speed railways, to CERN's particle accelerators, to triple-A video games.

In my free time, I love the Big 3 B's: brewing, bouldering & board games. Other than that, I like reading, trying to keep up with my dog, Maggie (pictured above) and going to the theatre.

Why become a developer?

I love solving problems, whether that's how to build a massive tunnel under a city centre, how to release an indie game hit or building robots to carry out inspections in radioactive spaces.

Learning full stack web development gives me more tools to solve more problems, and that can only be a good thing, right? I hope to use these skills to set up my own digital agency and help organisations tackle their challenges, big and small.
